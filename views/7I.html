Topic: Rustic Markets vs Modern Malls



Do you know what a Rustic Market is? 7I is reasearching about just that.





Advantages of Rustic Markets



Rustic Markets were mainly present in the mid 19th to 20th centuries. They are now slowly being phased out after the arrival of shopping malls. These markets are small shops where you can get almost every little thing from house decor, to stationery, to ceramic, etc. People in earlier days used to buy everything from here and these Markets were flourishing.



Advantages of Malls

Malls are huge modern day complexities. Just like Rustic Markets, You can get everything in under one roof, except for the huge amount in malls. Nowadays people go to shopping malls more than rustic markets and the laters are alowly being phased out.
